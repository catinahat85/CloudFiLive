Who Am I?

I'm a Cloud Engineer on a journey, with experience in Linux, GCP, AWS, Azure, OCI, Databricks, Generative AI, and Cloud Native Technologies.

I have a passion for learning and music and thought it would be helpful to combine both, since not all of us learn in the same way, have access to the same resources, and need extra tools in order to retain information

Learning Resources, Lofi Music
and Cloud Project Ideas

I will be sharing my learning resources, including ones I have used to learn and implement.

On my Youtube is where I will be sharing the creative works that I produced in tandem with my cloud skills and Generative AI. Many chill LoFi beats, but also study crams that I used, which I hope you find useful.

In addition to giving you a full view into my stack and workflow setup, there will be project ideas for you to gain the skills needed to become competent cloud or cloud-native engineers..
